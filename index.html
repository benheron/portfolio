Some things about me

I like to program, and I like to make games. Specifically, I like to know someone out there is getting joy from my work. I have a passion for games, from creating them, to playing them, to studying the design and how they work. The mechanics are equally as fascinating as the game theory behind the game itself. And I want other people to enjoy these things too.

I'm currently based in Plymouth, finishing my studies in Bsc Computing and Games Development. While at Uni, I have studied C++ and JavaScript, as well as used Java to work with Android. Prior studies include some extra experience with ActionScript and Visual Basic 6.

Away from the keyboard, you'll find me spending time at the Barbican or Hoe, or playing board games with friends.
